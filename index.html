About Us

Who are We??

Central Perk has long been seen in the famous sitcom F.R.I.E.N.D.S as the go-to cafe to get a great cup of coffee and quick bites and share moments with friends. Central Perk has now come to life with a new collection of artisanal coffees, dainty dollobs, creamy cheesecakes, buttery croissants, hearty donuts and wholesome ice-creams. The cafe delivers a rich, full taste with every portion.

Could it BE more amazing!!
We don’t think so.

So, give yourself a treat and grab a spot on the couch. You’re among friends.

We serve at--
New York City, California, Florida, Texas, Washington,Colorado, Hawaii, Massachusetts, Virginia, Arazona, Michigan, Georgia, Ohio.

The first Central Perk cafe was established in 2001 in the heart of New York City. After that many branches were opened in the tri-state area, followed by many other states across America!
